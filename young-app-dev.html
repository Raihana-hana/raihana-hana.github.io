Young App Developer, Yuma Soerianto

Singapore born, and has Indonesian family, Yuma Soerianto is an iOS App developer who lives in Melbourne, Australia. Yuma was started learn coding since 6 years old. He started coding with make website and online games for his friends to play. And now, Yuma develops App for iOS using Swift and Xcode.

Yuma got black belt in Taekwondo and he also plays piano. Yuma was the youngest Apple WWDC scholarship winner on 2017. He won the scholarship on 2018, 2019, and 2020 too. Yuma got many awards.
